# Code of Conduct
We follow the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2.1).

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Trolling or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Report issues to the maintainers listed in GOVERNANCE.md.
